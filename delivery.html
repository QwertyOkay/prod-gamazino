At Gamazino, we understand the excitement of receiving your gaming chair promptly. Our goal is to ensure that your new gaming throne reaches you with the speed and care it deserves. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

Shipping Options:

We offer a range of shipping options to cater to your needs:
Standard Shipping: Reliable delivery within 15-23 business days. Ideal for gamers who can't wait to get comfortable.
Express Shipping: Need your gaming chair sooner? Choose our express shipping option for delivery within 3-9 business days.

Shipping Costs:

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location, the selected shipping method, and the weight of your gaming chair. You can view the shipping cost during the checkout process before finalizing your order.

Order Processing:

We strive to process and ship your order as quickly as possible. Orders are typically processed within 5 business days. Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

International Shipping:

Good news for global gamers! We offer international shipping to bring gaming comfort to enthusiasts worldwide. Please note that international shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location and local customs regulations.

Estimated Delivery Times:

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the selected shipping method. Please refer to the estimated delivery time provided during the checkout process.

Tracking Your Order:

We provide tracking information for all shipped orders. You can track the progress of your delivery through the carrier's website using the provided tracking number.

Delivery Inspection:

Upon receiving your gaming chair, we recommend inspecting the packaging for any visible damage. If the packaging appears damaged, please make a note of it when signing for the delivery.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding shipping, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team at shipping@gamazino.com. We're here to assist you with any inquiries or assistance you may need.

Our Commitment:

At Gamazino, we're dedicated to ensuring that your gaming chair reaches you in pristine condition, ready to enhance your gaming adventures. Our commitment to a safe and efficient shipping process is unwavering.
