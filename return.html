Return Policy

We understand that purchasing a chair without trying it first can be challenging, so we offer a hassle-free 14-day return policy for all of our chairs. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it for a refund or exchange within 14 days of the delivery date.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for the return. Our team will provide you with a prepaid shipping label to send the item(s) back to us.

To be eligible for a refund or exchange, the item(s) must be in their original condition and packaging, with all tags and labels attached. The item(s) must be unused and free from any signs of wear or tear. Any items that are not in their original condition or are missing parts will not be eligible for a refund or exchange.

Once we receive the returned item(s) and verify their condition, we will process your refund or exchange within 5 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment. If you choose to exchange your item, we will ship the new item(s) to you as soon as possible.

Please note that we do not cover the cost of return shipping, and the customer is responsible for shipping the item(s) back to us. We also do not accept returns on custom orders or items that have been modified or altered in any way, unless there is a defect or error on our part.

We strive to provide our customers with the best possible shopping experience, and our return policy is a reflection of that commitment.
