At Gamazino, we want you to be absolutely satisfied with your gaming chair purchase. If, for any reason, you're not completely happy with your chair, we're here to make the return process straightforward and hassle-free. Please review our return policy below:

Eligibility for Returns:

We accept returns under the following conditions:
The return request is initiated within 16 days of receiving your order.
The gaming chair is in its original, unused condition, including all parts, accessories, and packaging.

How to Initiate a Return:

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
Contact our customer support team at returns@gamazino.com to request a return authorization (RA) number.
Provide your order number and a brief description of the reason for the return.
Once you receive your RA number, securely package the gaming chair with all its components and original packaging.

Return Shipping:

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a product defect or an error on our part. We recommend using a trackable and insured shipping method to ensure the safe return of the chair.

Inspection and Processing:

Upon receiving your return, our team will inspect the gaming chair to ensure it meets our return eligibility criteria. Once approved, we will process your return, and you will receive a refund or exchange based on your preference.

Refunds and Processing Time:

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase. Please allow 4 business days for the refund to be processed after we receive your returned gaming chair.

Damaged or Defective Products:

If you receive a damaged or defective gaming chair, please contact us at returns@gamazino.com within 14 days of receiving the order. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy or need assistance with a return, please contact our dedicated customer support team at returns@gamazino.com. We are here to help and ensure your gaming comfort.

Our Commitment:

At Gamazino, we're passionate about enhancing your gaming experience. Our return policy reflects our dedication to providing you with the best possible support and ensuring that your gaming chair meets your comfort expectations.

Thank you for choosing Gamazino as your gaming chair provider. We look forward to assisting you with any returns or inquiries you may have and continuing to elevate your gaming comfort.
